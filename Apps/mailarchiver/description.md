# Mail Archiver

MailArchiver connects to your IMAP mailbox and archives emails to a local searchable database. Full-text search, attachment extraction, and timeline views — your email history stays under your control.

**First steps:** Open `http://<host>:5000` and add your IMAP account credentials to start the initial sync.

**Data:** `/DATA/PowerLabAppData/mailarchiver/` — PostgreSQL database with archived emails and extracted attachments.
