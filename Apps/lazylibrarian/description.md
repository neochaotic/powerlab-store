# Lazylibrarian

LazyLibrarian is a book, magazine, and audiobook manager that monitors your reading wishlist and automatically downloads new releases via Usenet or BitTorrent. Integrates with Calibre and Readarr.

**First steps:** Open `http://<host>:5299` and configure your download clients and metadata providers.

**Data:** `/DATA/PowerLabAppData/lazylibrarian/` — SQLite database with book metadata, download history, and library links.
