# MeTube

MeTube is a self-hosted yt-dlp web UI for downloading videos and audio from YouTube, Vimeo, and 1000+ other sites. Supports playlists, format selection, subtitle download, and a download queue.

**First steps:** Open `http://<host>:8081`, paste a URL, choose your format, and hit Download.

**Data:** `/DATA/PowerLabAppData/metube/` — Downloaded media files and yt-dlp cookies.
