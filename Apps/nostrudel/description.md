# noStrudel

Nostr.club is a feature-rich Nostr client running in the browser. Supports notes, reactions, zaps (Lightning payments), DMs, long-form content, and relay management. Connects directly to your chosen Nostr relays.

**First steps:** Open `http://<host>:80` and log in with your Nostr private key (nsec) or a browser extension (Alby, nos2x).

**Data:** `/DATA/PowerLabAppData/nostrudel/` — Stateless — all data is fetched from Nostr relays.
