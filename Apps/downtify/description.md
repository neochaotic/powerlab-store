# Downtify

Downtify tracks availability and downtime for websites and services over time, storing the history locally. It provides a simple dashboard showing uptime percentages, response times, and incident timelines.

**First steps:** Open `http://<host>:8582` and add your first monitored endpoint.

**Data:** `/DATA/PowerLabAppData/downtify/` — SQLite database with uptime history and service configuration.
