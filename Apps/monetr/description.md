# Monetr

Monetr is a budgeting and personal finance tool focused on forward-looking cash flow — it shows when upcoming bills will hit your account and whether your balance can cover them. Connects to bank accounts via Plaid.

**First steps:** Open `http://<host>:4000` and create your account. Connect a bank account via Plaid or enter transactions manually.

**Data:** `/DATA/PowerLabAppData/monetr/` — PostgreSQL database with account data, transactions, and budget rules.
