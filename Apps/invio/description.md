# Invio

Invio is a self-hosted invoice and expense tracking tool for freelancers and small businesses. Create professional invoices, track payments, and manage clients — without sending data to a SaaS provider.

**First steps:** Open `http://<host>:8000` and register your account.

**Data:** `/DATA/PowerLabAppData/invio/` — SQLite database with invoices, clients, and payment records.
