# HortusFox

HortusFox is a self-hosted plant management app for tracking your plant collection — watering schedules, fertilizing logs, repotting history, and plant health notes. For green-fingered homelab owners.

**First steps:** Open `http://<host>:80` and create your account.

**Data:** `/DATA/PowerLabAppData/hortusfox/` — SQLite database with plant records and care history.
