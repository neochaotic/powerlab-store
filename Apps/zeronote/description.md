# ZeroNote

ZeroNote is a self-hosted, end-to-end encrypted note-taking app. Notes are encrypted client-side before leaving your browser — even the server operator cannot read them. Supports Markdown and notebook organization.

**First steps:** Open `http://<host>:8000` and register your account. Your encryption key is derived from your password.

**Data:** `/DATA/PowerLabAppData/zeronote/` — Encrypted note ciphertext (plaintext never touches the server).
