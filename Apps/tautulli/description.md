# Tautulli

Tautulli is the ultimate Plex analytics tool. Track who is watching what, when, and on which device. Generates detailed history reports, notification alerts (stream limits, new content), and playback statistics.

**First steps:** Open `http://<host>:8181` and connect it to your Plex server using a Plex token.

**Data:** `/DATA/PowerLabAppData/tautulli/` — SQLite database with Plex play history, user stats, and notification rules.
