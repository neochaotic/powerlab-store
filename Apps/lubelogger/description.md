# LubeLogger

LubeLogger is a vehicle maintenance and fuel log tracker. Record oil changes, tire rotations, fuel fill-ups, and repairs — complete with cost tracking and service reminders. Supports multiple vehicles.

**First steps:** Open `http://<host>:8080` and add your first vehicle.

**Data:** `/DATA/PowerLabAppData/lubelogger/` — SQLite database with vehicle records, maintenance logs, and fuel history.
